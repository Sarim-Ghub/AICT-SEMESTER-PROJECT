About shajar

At shajar, we're on a mission to transform the way businesses approach carbon offsetting. We believe that every company has the power to make a real difference in the fight against climate change.

WHO WE ARE

Our story

shajar was born from a shared concern about the future of our planet. We saw that many businesses wanted to reduce their carbon footprint but were held back by unclear, unreliable options.

The lack of transparency and verifiable results made it hard to take meaningful action. Determined to find a better way, we combined our expertise in sustainability and technology to create shajar.

We wanted to offer a solution that not only helps the environment but also provides businesses with clear proof of their impact. By pinpointing the exact tree responsible for each kilogram of absorbed CO2, we make carbon offsetting tangible and trustworthy.

VERIFIED TREE PLANTING

Backed by Science and Verified Standards

At shajar, we prioritize transparency and accountability, which is why we developed our own certification method, the Global Carbon Standard (GCS). Unlike existing standards like Verra and Gold Standard, our system ensures maximum transparency and traceability, with all data securely recorded in an immutable system.
