OUR SOLUTION

shajar has been accepted into the Science Based Targets initiative.

One of the major challenges in carbon offsetting is the risk of double counting, when the same CO2 offset is sold to multiple buyers. shajar eliminates this risk through the use of blockchain. Each carbon debit is unique and tied directly to a specific portion of carbon absorption, ensuring that your offset is counted only once and cannot be claimed by anyone else. Additionally, blockchain technology ensures that no fraudulent activity can take place. Since all data is immutable, no one can alter the information after it has been recorded, making your carbon offset investment secure and trustworthy.
