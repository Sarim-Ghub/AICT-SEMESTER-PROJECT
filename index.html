OUR ACHIEVEMENTS

Carbify has been accepted into the Science Based Targets initiative.

Carbify is now officially part of the Science Based Targets initiative (SBTi), a framework helping companies align climate goals with scientific research and the Paris Agreement. This milestone strengthens Carbify's commitment to sustainability by enhancing trust, transparency, and credibility in carbon offsetting and reforestation.
